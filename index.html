About our company

We want our teas to have more than just great flavor.

Great tea should also have superb health properties and be high in quality. We also treasure our community and our environment. Therefore, we put forth our best efforts to practise sustainable and green activities for the sake of our community and natural environment. We want to make a difference through sharing our love for tea and tea culture.
